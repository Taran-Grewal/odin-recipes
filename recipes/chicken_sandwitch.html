Chicken Sandwitch

Ingrediants

  • slices chicken ham
  • tablespoon butter
  • tablespoon butter
  • 2 slices cheese slices
  • 4 pieces lettuce leaf
  • 2 small sliced tomato
  • 4 slices white bread

Procedure

  1. In a grilling pan, brush some butter and then slightly grill all the pieces of bread. Once done, keep them aside.
  2. Now, apply butter on all the pieces of bread. You can customize the quantity as per your choice.
  3. Layer the bread Place one lettuce leaf on the bread and then add tomato slices. Top it with a slices of chicken ham and then cheese. Finally, add one more lettuce leaf and top it with another slice of bread. Grill the sandwich for 5minutes.
  4. Your Chicken Ham Sandwich is ready Your Chicken Ham Sandwich is ready. Enjoy with tomato sauce or your favorite dip.
